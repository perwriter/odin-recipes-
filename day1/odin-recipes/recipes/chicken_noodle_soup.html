Easy Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Easy Chicken Noodle Soup

Warm up with this classic chicken noodle soup. It's quick, easy, and perfect for chilly days.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced
  • 1 cup celery, chopped
  • 2 cups egg noodles
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, combine chicken broth, cooked chicken, carrots, celery, and thyme. Bring to a boil.
  2. Add egg noodles and cook until noodles are tender.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy this comforting soup!
